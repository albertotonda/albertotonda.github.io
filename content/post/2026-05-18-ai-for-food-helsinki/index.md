I was invited by Professor [Jiaheng Lu](https://www.cs.helsinki.fi/u/jilu/) of the [University of Helsinki](https://www.helsinki.fi/en/faculty-science/faculty/computer-science) to give a talk at the [AI Meets Food Science](https://www.cs.helsinki.fi/u/jilu/AIWorkshop) workshop. It was extremely interesting to hear recurring themes across all presentations, such as dealing with limited amount of training data and introducing codified expert knowledge in machine learning models. I particulary enjoyed the talk by Professor [Jack Yang](https://www.wur.nl/en/persons/dr-j-jack-yang) from Wageningen University, The Netherlands, as we shared several research topics related to food processing.
